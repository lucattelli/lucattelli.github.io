About Hostnames

August 11, 2021

Giving your machine a good hostname is important. This small gesture demonstrates how much you care about your setup. If you don’t know your hostname, you’ll have a hard time remotely connecting to it. Also, when using tmux, people will have to look at that weird name for a long time.

Have respect for your machine. Give it a good hostname.
