What to ask during the interview process

December 29, 2021
  • Company goal/mission?
  • Company stage and size?
  • Who’s the CEO (product, sales, engineering?)
  • Technology stack?
  • PTO policy?
  • Tell me more about the company culture?
  • How decisions are made?
  • How new ideas are introduced?
  • How you ensure the quality of your software?
  • How you ensure the security of your software?
  • How you ensure the scalability of your software?
  • How frequent do you ship to production?
  • How is software delivered?
  • How you deal with regressions?
  • How (and how often) do you collect user input and feedback?
  • How are teams organized?
  • How the services are organized?
  • How technical debt is managed?
  • What’s the core hours it’s expected to be online for synchronous collaboration with the team?
  • Is there a performance review process in place? How does it work?
  • How frequent will I get feedback on what I’m doing?
  • How does the company help me learn new things (for example, personal stipend, available online learning platforms, etc)
  • Tell me about a hard decision you had to make recently
  • Tell me about a highlight while working at this company
  • Tell me about a lowlight while working at this company
  • How is support done?
  • How much data driven is the company?
